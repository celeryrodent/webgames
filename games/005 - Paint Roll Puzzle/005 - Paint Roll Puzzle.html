How to play
Roll the paint ball using arrow keys or swipe. The ball rolls until it hits a wall. Paint every tile exactly once and reach the exit. Don't retrace — you must paint each tile only once.
Levels
Controls
Arrow keys/WASD to move. Swipe on touch. Use Undo to revert last move.
