004 - Starlight Sort
Level: 1   Score: 0
How to play
Swap adjacent stars (drag or click two). Match 3+ by color to clear. Bigger matches create shooting stars which clear a full row or spawn comet effects. Clear constellation patterns for bonus animations.
Objectives
Score points by matching stars. Complete level targets before the sky fills.
Next special
Make a T or L match to create a shooting star.
Controls
Drag on touch or mouse to swap. Tap to select then tap neighbor to swap.
