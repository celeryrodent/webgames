# Neon Jump

A fast-paced, addictive endless platformer with stunning neon visuals. Built for HTML5 and optimized for CrazyGames.

![Neon Jump](screenshot.png)

## 🎮 How to Play

### Controls
- **Move Left/Right**: Arrow Keys (← →) or A/D
- **Jump**: SPACE, Arrow Up (↑), or W
- **Mobile**: Touch the left/right/jump zones on screen

### Objective
- Jump on platforms to climb higher
- Collect yellow coins for bonus points
- Land on springs for super jumps
- Grab powerups for special abilities
- Don't fall off the screen!

## 🚀 Features

### Gameplay
- **Smooth 60fps gameplay**
- **Full left/right movement control**
- **Progressive difficulty scaling**
- **Combo system** - chain jumps for bonus points
- **High score saves locally**
- **Mobile-optimized touch controls**

### Visuals
- Neon glow effects and particle systems
- Animated background stars
- Player trail effects
- Pulsing coins and powerups
- Retro synthwave aesthetic

### Platform Types
- **Static** (Cyan) - Standard platforms
- **Moving** (Magenta) - Slide back and forth
- **Breakable** (Orange) - Break after one bounce

### Powerups
- **🚀 Jetpack** (Orange) - Super high jumps for 10 seconds
- **🛡️ Shield** (Green) - Can't die from falling for 10 seconds
- **🧲 Magnet** (Pink) - Attracts nearby coins for 10 seconds

### Collectibles
- **💰 Coins** - Worth 10 points each (+ combo bonus)
- **📊 Springs** - Launch you extra high (+20 points)

## 🎯 Scoring

- Gain points by climbing higher
- Collect coins for base points
- Combo multiplier: chain platform bounces for up to 5x bonus
- Springs give +20 points
- Beat your high score!

## 🛠️ Technical

- Pure HTML5 Canvas (no dependencies)
- ~30KB total size
- Works offline
- Responsive design
- Local storage for high scores

## 📱 Platforms

- Desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Mobile)
- CrazyGames integration ready
